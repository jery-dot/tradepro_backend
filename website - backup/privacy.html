Privacy Policy

Trade Pro (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our app or website.

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Information you provide: When you create an account, contact support, or use certain features, we may collect your name, email, phone number, company details, and other information you provide.
  • Usage information: We collect information about how you use the app, such as pages visited, actions taken, and device information.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: We may use cookies, analytics tools, and other tracking technologies to improve your experience and monitor usage.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Communicate with you, including support and updates
  • Analyse app usage and trends
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • With service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., hosting, analytics, payment processing)
  • To comply with legal requirements or protect our rights.

4. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct or update your information.
  • Request deletion of your information.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective Date” at the top will indicate the latest version.
