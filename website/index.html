About TradePro Services

America is facing a skilled labor crisis. Since the pandemic, millions of experienced tradespeople have left the workforce, many through early retirement, while fewer young workers are entering the trades to replace them. Today, entire industries—construction, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, manufacturing are struggling to fill essential roles, even as demand continues to grow. In some regions, there are nearly twice as many open skilled trade jobs as there are qualified workers to fill them.

At the same time, decades of hands-on knowledge are disappearing from job sites. When experienced tradespeople retire without passing on their skills, communities lose more than workers—they lose craftsmanship, mentorship, and self-reliance. Re-invigorating the trades isn't just about filling jobs; it's about rebuilding skill, purpose, and opportunity, especially for the next generation of workers who want meaningful, well-paid careers without taking on lifelong debt.

Designed for electricians, plumbers, HVAC technicians, welders, carpenters, and other skilled trade professionals, Trade Pro makes it easier to find jobs, hire trusted talent, and build lasting professional connections. By bridging opportunity, mentorship, and hands-on learning, Trade Pro supports every stage of a trade career—from apprentice to expert.

Platform Feature

Everything You Need to Find Work or Post Jobs

Easy Job Discovery

Find nearby trade jobs based on location, skills, wages, and availability. View complete job details including pay rate, duration, start date, and ratings before applying.

Ratings & Reviews

After each job, both labor and job posters can leave ratings and reviews. This system builds trust, improves job quality, and helps users make informed decisions.

Easy Job Post Management

Create, edit, or close job posts directly from the app.Track job status such as Pending, In Progress, Completed, or Cancelled for full transparency.

Smart Application Messaging

When a worker applies using Quick Apply, an automatic message is sent instantly. Review applications and respond directly through in-app chat.

 Apprenticeship Profiles

Apprentices can create profiles highlighting trade interests, education, and experience. Connect with trade professionals offering apprenticeship opportunities and mentorship.

Local Trade Marketplace

Buy and sell tools, equipment, materials, or businesses locally. Create listings, manage posts, and communicate directly with buyers or sellers.
